🛵 Travel Style

Train + Volvo + Scooty + Govt Buses + Walking Trails

💸 Estimated Budget

🌦️ Weather

Cool mornings, pleasant afternoons & possible rain showers in June

🧭 Full Day-Wise Itinerary

🚆 Day 1 – Hyderabad → Delhi

  • Regular day in Hyderabad
  • Final packing + snacks for train
  • Train No: 12707
  • Departure: 4:20 PM
  • Overnight train journey

🚌 Day 2 – Delhi → Manali

  • Arrival at Delhi around 6:35 PM
  • Metro to Kashmiri Gate ISBT
  • Overnight Volvo Bus to Manali
  • First mountain weather experience 🌲

🏔️ Day 3 – Settle Into Manali

  • Morning bus drop at Manali
  • Hotel check-in & rest
  • Acclimatization walk in Old Manali
  • Riverside chill + photography
  • Dinner at Tenzin Dhaba
  • Coffee & dessert at Dylan's Toasted & Roasted Coffee House

🛕 Day 4 – Temples + Hot Springs + Waterfall Hike

  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple
  • Forest photo walk nearby
  • Ride towards Vashisht
  • Vashisht Hot Water Springs
  • Jogini Falls hike
  • Carry rain layer & water bottle
  • Riverside dinner at Cafe 1947
  • Early sleep for next adventure day

🪂 Day 5 – Solang Valley Adventure Day

  • Early ride to Solang Valley
  • Paragliding (best early morning)
  • Zipline
  • ATV rides
  • Simple dhaba lunch
  • Optional chairlift ride/viewpoint walk
  • Evening dinner at Cafe 1986

🏔️ Day 6 – Atal Tunnel + Sissu + Rohtang Route

  • Early morning departure
  • Ride through Atal Tunnel
  • Rohtang route scenic viewpoints
  • Explore Sissu riverside valley
  • Optional Sissu Waterfall hike
  • Photography stops throughout ride
  • Dinner at Rocky's Cafe

🚣 Day 7 – River Rafting + Cafe Day

  • Morning Beas River rafting
  • Babeli–Pirdi stretch rafting route
  • Expected cost: ₹800–₹1500 per person
  • Quick dry clothes recommended
  • Carry waterproof phone pouch
  • Cafe hopping in Old Manali
  • Bakery stop at Me Garage Cafe & Bakery
  • Dinner at Monastery Cafe

🌲 Day 8 – Kasol + Manikaran Sahib

  • Govt bus from Manali to Kasol
  • Bike rental in Kasol
  • Parvati Valley viewpoints
  • Visit Manikaran Sahib
  • Langar & hot springs area
  • Evening dinner at Cafe By The Hidden Tribe
  • Overnight stay at Drifter's Inn & Cafe
  • Night bus to Delhi

🚆 Day 9 – Delhi → Hyderabad

  • Morning arrival in Delhi
  • Buffer time for traffic
  • Train back to Hyderabad
  • Relaxing overnight journey

🏁 Day 10 – Hyderabad Arrival

  • Train journey continues
  • Reach Hyderabad in evening
  • Trip ends 🎉

🧳 Packing Checklist

  • Rain jacket / poncho
  • Quick-dry clothes
  • Thermal layer for mornings/nights
  • Power bank
  • Medicines + ORS
  • Sports shoes for hikes
  • Waterproof pouch for rafting
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses
  • Offline Google Maps downloaded
  • Cash for remote areas

📌 Important Notes

  • Start mountain rides early morning
  • Avoid riding scooters after sunset
  • Rohtang route weather changes quickly
  • Keep buffer time for landslides or traffic
  • Always inspect rental scooty before taking
  • Carry original ID proofs
  • June is peak season — prebook buses/hotels
  • Keep one flexible backup day if weather changes
